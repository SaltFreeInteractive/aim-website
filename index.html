About

A.I.M. (Animal Insurgent Mercenaries) is an Advance Wars like military themed turn based combat strategy game featuring animal characters!

>: START TRANSMISSION

Blossom island is falling to a private military group named C.L.A.W (Counter Logistics Animal Warefare) led by their feline commander Rose Toge. With each sector of the island being lost to C.L.A.W. it is up to Ela, a mouse Navy Admiral, to stand her ground against the advancing enemy forces. Ela must work with any remaining allied animal soldiers, civilians and mercenaries to join forces, push back C.L.A.W. and retake blossom island!

>: END TRANSMISSION
